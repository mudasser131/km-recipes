Shawarma recipe

1. Marinate the Chicken:
In a bowl, combine 500 grams of boneless chicken, 1/4 cup of yogurt, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1 teaspoon of ground paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix well to coat the chicken. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. 2. Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). 3. Prepare the Shawarma Spice Blend: In a small bowl, combine 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1 teaspoon of ground paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper to taste.
4. Cook the Chicken: Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers. Sprinkle the shawarma spice blend evenly over the chicken skewers. Place the skewers on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
5. Prepare the Shawarma Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together 1/2 cup of tahini, 2 tablespoons of lemon juice, 2 tablespoons of water, 1 clove of minced garlic, and a pinch of salt.
6. Assemble the Shawarma: Warm pita bread or flatbreads in the oven. Remove the chicken from the skewers. Place the chicken on the warm pita or flatbread. Drizzle the shawarma sauce over the chicken. Add your choice of toppings, such as chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and fresh parsley.
7. Wrap and Serve: Fold the pita or flatbread over the chicken and toppings. Secure with a toothpick or wrap in foil. Serve your homemade shawarma hot and enjoy!
