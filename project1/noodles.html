Noodles recipe

recipe - Homemade Noodles description: Learn how to make delicious homemade noodles from scratch with this easy recipe. These fresh noodles are perfect for all your favorite pasta dishes. prepTime: 45 minutes cookTime: 2-4 minutes totalTime: 49-51 minutes servingSize: 4 servings ---
## Ingredients - 2 cups all-purpose flour - 2 large eggs - 1/2 teaspoon salt - 2-4 tablespoons water (if needed)
## Instructions 1. **Make the Noodle Dough:** - In a large mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour and make a well in the center. - Add eggs and salt into the well. - Gradually mix the eggs and flour until the dough starts coming together. Add water if needed to form a cohesive ball. 2. **Knead the Dough:** - Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. 3. **Rest the Dough:** - Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes at room temperature. 4. **Roll Out the Dough:** - Divide the dough into two portions. - Roll out one portion on a floured surface into a thin sheet, about 1/8 inch (3mm) thick. 5. **Cut the Noodles:** - Fold the dough a few times to create layers, then cut it into your desired noodle width. 6. **Cook the Noodles:** - Boil a large pot of salted water. - Add the freshly cut noodles and stir to prevent sticking. - Cook for 2-4 minutes until tender but slightly firm (al dente). 7. **Drain and Serve:** - Drain the noodles using a colander. - Serve with your favorite sauce or toppings.
## Notes - Customize your noodles by adjusting the thickness and width to your liking. - Fresh noodles cook quickly, so be attentive to avoid overcooking. Enjoy your homemade noodles!
**Tip:** You can get creative with different sauces and toppings to make a variety of delicious dishes.
